This is the heart of your module.

Common statuses:

Pending
Under Review
Approved
Rejected
Completed

💡 Each status should include:

Timestamp (date & time)
Assigned personnel (who handled it)
Remarks / comments
📌 Status Timeline / History
Show a step-by-step timeline
Example:
Submitted → Jan 10
Reviewed → Jan 11
Approved → Jan 12

Status
Date

Staff reviews → updates to Under Review
Staff decides → Approved / Rejected
Final → Completed
